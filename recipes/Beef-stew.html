Beef stew recipe

Here is a simple recipe on how to prepare beef stew.

You will need;

Ingredients

  • 1kg beef
  • 3 potatoes, chopped
  • 1 chopped onion
  • 2 carrots
  • 2 tbps. cooking oil
  • 2 tbsp. plain flour
  • 2 tbsp. tomato paste
  • Beef stock cubes crumbled

Instructions for preparing

  1. Cut your beef into pieces and wash.
  2. Add 2 tbsp of cooking oil in a pot and heat.
  3. Add the beef pieces one by one and sprinkle some salt.
  4. Brown the beef on each side and remove the pot.
  5. Put the chopped onion and seasoning in the pot and stir for about 3 minutes.
  6. Add the tomato paste and mix well.
  7. Add the beef and potatoes with hot water and cook for about 20 minutes.
  8. The n stir in 2 tbsp of plain flour, 2 tbsp of tomato paste and crumbled beef stock cubes.
  9. Cook until the meat is tender and bring to a gentle simmer.
  10. The beef stew should have a thick gravy by now.
  11. Ready to serve.

Can be enjoyed with;

  • Nshima
  • Rice
